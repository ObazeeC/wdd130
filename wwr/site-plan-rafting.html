Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to design a user appealing website, very easy to use and inviting. It is to bring rafting sites closer to a variety of individual.

Audience

Who are the the target:The target audience are adults, couples with or without kids.
What do they want: The target audience wants to see the reality of the a rafting site, they want good images, and videos of individuals who have previously visited the site.
How will will my audience usually access my site: From any devices. Both the site will be more appealing with desktops devices or laptops.
